Social Media As
It Should Be

Joyfill is a social media application that cultivates happiness into people's lives by helping you evaluate and invest in joys with the people you care about.

Key Features

List Your Joys

Find what truly brings happiness to yourself and your closest friends. Document them to be mindful of your values and understand what social needs need to be met.

Fulfill Your Desires

Commit to activities that are meaningful to you and be reminded to complete them.

Share Your Joys

Share your joys with your connections and capture the moments that matter.
